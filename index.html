About me

Colorado based aspiring developer who focuses his time on creating something out of unique thoughts, spending time with his wife and dogs, or in the outdoors climbing a mountain.

I have a strong passion for serving and helping others that has led me to many pursuits in hopes of finding where I can make a difference.

    In the last decade
  • Enlisted 4 years in the United States Marine Corps, with 2 combat deployments to Afghanistan to help mitigate Improvised Explosive Device threat.
  • Built high rises in Seattle, Washington as a Carpenter Apprentice.
  • Obtained a Bachelors in Environmental Policy and Management to learn about the increasing need for sustainability and how to ameloriate the damage humans do to the Earth.
  • Became a certified Single-pitch Rock Climbing instructor, spending 2 years climbing in California, Washington, and Canada; volunteer guiding with a Veteran non-profit in Colorado and Washington, and found myself eventually working with at-risk youth and disabled populations in Lakewood, Colorado.
  • Finally, for the past year, I have worked for a startup called Oh My Green in various roles to help establish a presence in Denver, Colorado and spread the mission of a healthier, happier, more productive workplace with the help of organic, sustainable, and non-gmo foods.
